Features

Everything a password manager should do — plus a face

Core vault functionality you'd expect, with biometric authentication built in from the start.

Face recognition login

Unlock your vault with a facial scan powered by FaceNet embeddings. No password to remember or type.

Encrypted vault

Every credential is stored securely and scoped to your account only. Nothing is shared between users.

Anti-spoofing built in

A dedicated liveness model checks for real faces before matching, so a photo or screen won't pass.

Email OTP backup

Can't use the camera? A one-time code sent to your inbox gets you into your vault just as fast.

Browser extension

Autofill saved credentials on any site after a quick face check, right from your browser toolbar.

Session-scoped access

Every session expires automatically, and each verification is tied to your account and yours alone.
